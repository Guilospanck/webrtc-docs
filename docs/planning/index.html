Planning

When planning constraints and things like that:

  • Choose the right codec.
  • Focus on what your application actually uses.
  • Use the Pareto principle and act on the lower hanging fruits first. Things to be aware: frequency that something happens, how important it is, complexity, effort. In other words: ROI (return on investment).
